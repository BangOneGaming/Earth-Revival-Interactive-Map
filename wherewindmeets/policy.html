Privacy and Policy

Welcome to our website. Your privacy is important to us. Please read the following policy carefully to understand how your data is handled.

1. Purpose of Login

The login system on this website provides access to features such as:

  • Posting and viewing comments.
  • Tracking markers that you have visited on the map.
  • Receiving notifications from the website.
  • Connecting with other players in "Where Winds Meet".
  • Identifying users by username (not from the game) to know who provides feedback or posts comments.

Note: This login is not connected to your game account. Your game login credentials remain completely secure and private. The username collected is only for website identification purposes and has no connection to in-game usernames.

2. Data Collection

When you log in using this system, we may store the following information:

  • Your email and basic profile info from your Google account (used for authentication).
  • Username for website identification (not related to the game account).
  • Comments you post on the website.
  • Markers you have visited or interacted with on the map.
  • Notification preferences.

3. Data Usage

Collected data will only be used to:

  • Provide personalized features on the map and website.
  • Enable comment functionality and interaction with other players.
  • Identify users for feedback and comment purposes.
  • Send notifications relevant to your activity on the website.

4. Data Security

We take appropriate measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. Your game account information is never accessed or stored by this system.

Where Winds Meet – All Regions on the Map

The Where Winds Meet map covers all major explorable regions:

  • Kaifeng – Kaifeng City, Song Dynasty Palace, Grand Canal, Ever-Normal Granary, Ghost Market, Jinming Pool
  • Qinghe – Verdant Wilds, Moonveil Mountain, Mercyheart Town, Thousand Buddha Village, Jadebrook Mountain
  • Bujian Mountain – Bamboo Abode, Sundara Land, mountain caves and hidden paths
  • Hexi – Yumen Pass, Liangzhou (with Time Travel feature), Qinchuan
  • Hutuo – Yunqiu, Luancheng, Hutuo South Bank, Fenglong Mountain
  • Royal Palace – Coming soon

Both surface and underground layers are supported, with floor selection for cave systems and dungeons.

Frequently Asked Questions – Where Winds Meet Map

Is this the official Where Winds Meet Interactive Map?

This is a fan-made interactive map created by BangOne Gaming, not an official product. It is the most complete community map available for Where Winds Meet exploration.

How do I find all Treasure Chests in Where Winds Meet?

Enable the Treasure Chest filter on the map to see all chest locations across every region. Puzzle Chests use a separate icon since they don't appear on the in-game minimap.

Does the Where Winds Meet map work on mobile?

Yes. The map works on mobile browsers. For the best experience, enable Desktop Mode in your mobile browser settings.

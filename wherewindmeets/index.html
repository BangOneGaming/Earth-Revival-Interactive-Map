Where Winds Meet Interactive Map – Complete Exploration Guide

The Where Winds Meet Interactive Map is the most complete exploration tool for players who want to fully discover the world of Jianghua.

Where Winds Meet Map – All Location Types

  • Teleport Landmarks – Fast travel points across all regions
  • Treasure Chests – Common chests and Puzzle Chests hidden across Jianghua
  • World Bosses – Locations of all major boss encounters
  • Cat Play & Meow Meow Treasure – Find every cat interaction spot
  • Wander Tales – Complete all roaming story quests
  • Tales and Echoes – Discover hidden narrative locations
  • Fishing Spots – All fishing locations across rivers and lakes
  • Mini-Games – Pitch Pot, Miaodao card game, Archer Contest, Sumo Wrestling, and Graceful Melody

Where Winds Meet – All Regions on the Map

  • Kaifeng – Kaifeng City, Song Dynasty Palace, Grand Canal, Ever-Normal Granary, Ghost Market, Jinming Pool
  • Qinghe – Verdant Wilds, Moonveil Mountain, Mercyheart Town, Thousand Buddha Village, Jadebrook Mountain
  • Bujian Mountain – Bamboo Abode, Sundara Land, mountain caves and hidden paths
  • Hexi – Yumen Pass, Liangzhou (with Time Travel feature), Qinchuan
  • Hutuo – Yunqiu, Luancheng, Hutuo South Bank, Fenglong Mountain
